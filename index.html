Welcome to Movie Meetup!

My name is Sudhamsh Mondrati. I'm the CEO and founder of Movie Meetup, a place where you can find and go to movie meetup events. You can even add your own movie meetup so others can join you.

Ever since high school, I've always wanted to watch movies, not just by myself but with my friends. For me, it was a great experience to be with friends and enjoy watching movies so we could have a great time and have fun!

Check out our current events and join one now! If you haven't already, go ahead and sign up and log in if you want to create your own event for others to join!
